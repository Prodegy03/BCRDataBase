About Book Content and Rating Database

We are here to help you know the general content of what you will read before you open the pages of the book. It is never fun to be reading a book to get half way through and hit that point when the author decided to slip something in that you don't agree with. Then you have the option of either putting the book down or putting up with another half of a book with content periodically that you don't want to read. THat is frustrating. We are also here to help you know what content your children or other dependents are reading.
